## INTERACTIONS & STATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

- Side drawer: opens/closes on hamburger click 
  (slide-in from right with dark overlay behind)
- Buy/Sell toggle: swaps green button label between 
  "Buy BTC" and "Sell BTC", and colors the active tab
- Limit/Market tab: shows/hides price input field
- Order form % buttons: update amount field proportionally
- Markets search: filters coin list in real-time
- Markets filter tabs (All/Gainers/Losers): 
  filters coin list
- Star toggle: fills/unfills star icon on click
- Deposit stepper: clicking Continue advances 
  from step 1 → 2 → 3, Back goes backward
- Cryptocurrency dropdown in deposit: opens/closes list
- Toast notification on dashboard: 
  appears on load, auto-dismisses after 3 seconds
- Ticker bar: infinite CSS animation scrolling left
